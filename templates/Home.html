Canteen at our school

At Fairvale High School, our canteen is committed to supporting the health and wellbeing of our students. We follow the NSW Healthy School Canteens Strategy, which means we provide a wide range of nutritious food and drink options to help students make healthy choices every day.

Our menu is designed to be both delicious and balanced – featuring fresh fruit, wholesome meals, and snacks that fuel learning and activity. We aim to make the healthy choice the easy choice for everyone.

We’re proud to offer a canteen that promotes better learning, energy, and focus throughout the school day.

Visit Healthy School Canteens to learn more.
